African Delicacies

Egwusi soup and it's Health benefits

Egwusi soup is one of the delicious meal popularly eaten in West Africa.It has high nutritional values. It contains fiber, potassium, magnessium etc

Method of cooking

  • Grinding of melon
  • Boiling of meat and stock fish
  • When the meat softens, add a little palm oil in it and mould your melon in little portions and put it inside the pot
  • Allow to cook for some minutes and then add your vegetable of your choice.
